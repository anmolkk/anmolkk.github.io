Hinglish Dictionary

Hinglish Dictionary is a Python Django Based Web Dictionary, which is Used to Search for the meaning of Hinglish Word into Particular English Word. This Dictionary has been deployed over the Web. In Hinglish Dictionary, we also Provide suggestion if the searched word is not present in Dictionary. Users can also provide new suggested words.

Traveller

Traveller is a Python Django Based Blog Website to help Travel Lovers. At Traveller, We Provide you the Opportunity to look for the Places to plan your Next Vacations. You can Plan Your Next Vacation By Choosing the Best Places Near Delhi from Our Home Page. You can also search for the Places of your Choice from the Search Bar. You can check the Travel Distance from Delhi to that Place. You can Also Rent Bikes in Traveller through our Parter Website.

SocialPedia

SocialPedia, A MERN Stack Based Social media Website where we can add friends, upload pictures, share posts, and do a lot of things. Anyone can Create their Account in SocialPedia. In SocialPedia, you can change light and Dark Mode.
